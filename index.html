ATMERION

Independent Scientific & Technical Advisory

Atmerion provides independent scientific and technical advisory services for organizations working with complex environmental and physical systems, where uncertainty, assumptions, and interpretation materially affect decisions.

Areas of advisory work

  • Independent review of atmospheric and environmental risk analyses
  • Assessment of uncertainty, assumptions, and model limitations
  • Second-opinion reviews of internal and external technical reports
  • Interpretation of atmospheric and climate information for decision-makers
  • Scientific input for due diligence and risk communication

Leadership

Atmerion is led by Marat Khairoutdinov, PhD, Professor of Atmospheric Science with more than 30 years of experience in numerical modeling, atmospheric dynamics, and analysis of weather and climate systems.

Atmerion provides advisory and analytical services only. It does not offer operational forecasting, software development, or regulatory filings.
