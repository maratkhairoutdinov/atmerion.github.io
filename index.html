ATMERION

Independent Scientific & Technical Advisory

Atmerion provides independent scientific and technical advisory services for organizations operating in complex environmental and physical systems. The focus is on risk-aware analysis, interpretation, and review of atmospheric and environmental information used in decision-making.

Areas of advisory work

  • Independent review of environmental and atmospheric risk analyses
  • Assessment of assumptions, uncertainty, and limitations in models and data
  • Second-opinion technical reviews for internal or external reports
  • Interpretation of atmospheric and climate information for non-specialists
  • Technical support in due diligence and risk communication

Leadership

Atmerion is led by Marat Khairoutdinov, PhD, Professor of Atmospheric Science with more than 25 years of experience in numerical modeling, atmospheric dynamics, and analysis of weather and climate systems.

Atmerion provides advisory and analytical services only. It does not offer operational forecasting, software development, or regulatory filings.
